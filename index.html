What We Build

National infrastructure
for the real economy

We do not build apps. We build the systems that financial institutions, regulators, governments and security agencies run on — designed to last 30 years, operate at national scale and generate sovereign value.

01
Financial Systems
UBL and NFEIS give Ghana's financial sector the behavioural intelligence, inclusion infrastructure and regulatory visibility it has never had before.
02
Security Systems
AKOMA-NIS is a 30-year border intelligence architecture — biometrics, AI threat detection, trade integrity and post-quantum security in one sovereign system.

Social Systems
AYL and EduTrack build the employability and education data infrastructure that turns Ghana's youth into its most tracked, scored and bankable asset.
